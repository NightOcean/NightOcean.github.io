    My Project Idea

    Overview

    As for a project idea that I am willing to create, I like to develop a game with the title of it being temporary called “Soul Companion” with it being in a format of a Pixel Role Playing Game with a mix of puzzle and explorations. However, the player you control in the game will have self-awareness, knowing that you exist to control them and that what you do determines their outcome or so to say, fate.

    Motivation

    This would be an interesting project to take as I have prior experiences in making games using GameMaker or Clickteam Fusion. While I have not created a full game before, I have done small prototypes in the past where I did every role by myself such as creating the stage or level, creating the sprite & animations, creating sound & music, play test, debugging errors and trying to motivate myself to do all of this for the desire to enjoy playing games as a gamer, which I believe is a well worth commitment to take. As a solo enthusiastic game developer, I just love to try out things in every perspective regardless of if it is easy or difficult.
    I believe that making this game can be considered as playing the game at the same time, as you are developing the story in a way that you would enjoy it as both a developer and gamer’s perspective.

    Description

    The project “Soul Companion” is a game program that will be made using Game Maker and would be operated on Windows OS as a .exe file, with potential future options for porting to MacOS and Linux, once the initial development is complete.

    The user finds themselves controlling the player in a garbage dump that would seem to be silent at first but will later start to communicate more and open up to the user as you journey together in a mysterious devastated world with a population less than a few hundred thousand.

    The user will be given no plot directions outside of just instructions on how to play the game. The game will simply, or more specifically the player will tell you that they will unconsciously want to interact more with you and the objects or people around them. If there is a main focus, then it would be that you, the user must help the player survive.

    You can move the player through key inputs such as ASWD and will be able to use action or special commands that the user can ask the player to do or respond, such as interacting with objects or people. The user will also have the option to rekey bind their controls depending on their preferences in the menu settings.

    Action commands can be like interact, accept, decline, and will depend on the situation you are in the game.

    An example of a special command is that you can ask the player how they are feeling right now, or to dance to the background music that only you can hear.

    In this game, there is no such thing as game overs. So, when you come across a difficult situation, the player will prompt the user to just retry into they complete it. However, there will be situations where your choices are permanent, and you will never be able to go back again unless you start from the very beginning. These choices will not really cause a grave error on your end and will not affect your overall experience with the player.

    While the target audience is for anybody who enjoy games in general, it is aimed towards those who enjoy solving puzzles and being a part of the story that would test the bonds between the user and the player. You will also enjoy this game if you like to interact with the player and help them solve their problem.

    I like to mention that there is actually no story given but more like you imagine your own story which is the journey you have with the player as you travel from place to place.
